Reference & Guides

How to Use

  1. Choose Question Type: On the home page, select either "Standard" or "Node-Based" question.
  2. Prepare Your Files: Use the outline templates linked below to prepare your `.lua` config and `.json` test case files.
  3. Upload Files: On the preparation page, upload your completed `.lua` and `.json` files.
  4. Generate & Download: Click the "Generate & Download JSON" button to receive your final `coding_questions.json` file.

Tool Features

  • Dual Question Type Support: Generate JSON for both Standard and Node-Based (Linked List, Binary Tree) questions.
  • Direct File Uploads: No more copy-pasting. Directly upload your `.lua` and `.json` files.
  • Automatic Weightage Calculation: Test case weights are calculated automatically based on question difficulty.
  • Comprehensive Parsing: Handles all metadata, hints, follow-up questions, and language-specific debug code.
  • One-Click Generation: Instantly generates and downloads the final `coding_questions.json` file.
